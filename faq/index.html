Frequently Asked Questions

What is AGDA?

We are a student-run game development club comprised of programmers, artists, writers, designers, and musicians. Through us, you can form a team with members of different disciplines and plan your own projects! We also host workshops, game nights, and other fun events to expand your game development horizons.

What types of events do you host?

We host a variety of events which include weekly team meetings, workshops, socials, game nights and so much more.

When does the club meet?

We meet every Tuesday and Thursday from 5-8pm.

Do you need experience?

None what so ever. No experience is needed as there will be execs and workshops along the way to help you.
